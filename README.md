# Sword Coast Homebrew

A collection of homebrew content for D&D 5e. Primarily Forgotten Realms content with the Sword Coast as the main focus, but not limited to it. Includes original monsters, house rules, and supplemental systems designed to add depth, realism, and memorable moments to your table.

All content here is original unless otherwise noted. Free to use, adapt, and share.

---

## ⚠️ Content Warnings

Some content in this repository deals with mature or sensitive themes. Each directory and file includes its own warning where applicable, but as a general heads-up:

- **Monsters** may involve psychological horror, grief, and emotional manipulation
- **Rules** include systems covering pregnancy, age of consent across fantasy races, and mature roleplay scenarios

This content is intended for **adult tables** or tables where all players have given informed consent. Always discuss at Session Zero.

---

## Contents

### 📁 [monsters/](./monsters/README.md)
Original homebrew monsters. Currently includes:
- **The Beloved** — A shapeshifting aberration that takes the form of whoever you love most. Psychological horror, CR 5.
- **Jay Bird** — Adorable. Charming. Will peck your fingers off. CR 1/8 (individual) / CR 2 (swarm).

### 📁 [rules/](./rules/README.md)
House rules and supplemental systems. Currently includes:
- **Age of Maturity Guide** — Legal and social adulthood by race across the Forgotten Realms.
- **Pregnancy System** — Optional mechanical system for pregnancy and childbirth.
- **Rule of Cool** — A framework for allowing creative, cinematic actions outside the standard rules.
