# Monsters

Original homebrew monsters for D&D 5e. Each monster lives in its own directory to allow for supporting files (tokens, maps, handouts) to be added over time.

---

## ⚠️ Content Warning

Some monsters in this collection involve **psychological horror**, themes of **grief and loss**, and **emotional manipulation**. These are intended to create memorable, dramatic encounters — but may be distressing for some players. Always discuss sensitive content at Session Zero and have safety tools (X-card, etc.) available.

---

## Index

### [The Beloved](./the_beloved/the_beloved.md)
*Medium aberration (shapechanger), neutral evil — CR 7*

A creature that reads the deepest emotional attachments of those it encounters and transforms into whoever they love most — a dead spouse, a missing child, a lost comrade. Unlike a Boggart which weaponises fear, the Beloved weaponises love. Nearly impossible to fight something wearing your dead wife's face.

Includes full stat block, tactics, encounter design notes, variants (CR 3 / CR 6 / CR 7 / CR 8), adventure hooks, and extensive DM guidance on running it safely and meaningfully.

> ⚠️ **Sensitive themes:** grief, loss, emotional trauma, psychological horror. Strongly recommended to flag at Session Zero.

### [Jay Bird](./jay_bird/jay_bird.md)
*Tiny beast, unaligned — CR 1/8 (individual) / CR 2 (swarm)*

Inspired by VLDL D&D. Adorable, iridescent birds with a supernatural charm effect that makes them seem harmless. They are not harmless. A lone bird is a curiosity; a swarm is a nightmare that will permanently relieve you of your fingers, toes, ears, and eyes.

Includes individual and swarm stat blocks, a permanent appendage loss table, encounter design, ecology, adventure hooks, and variants.

> No significant content warnings, though the **permanent maiming mechanic** should be discussed with players before use.
